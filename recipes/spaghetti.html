Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish made with long, thin noodles that are typically boiled until tender and then tossed in a rich, flavorful sauce. One of the most popular versions features a savory tomato-based sauce simmered with garlic, onions, herbs, and sometimes meat or vegetables. Topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and garnished with basil or parsley, spaghetti offers a comforting, satisfying meal that's loved around the world. Its simplicity, versatility, and bold flavors make it a timeless favorite for both quick weeknight dinners and traditional family gatherings.
