Chicken Sandwich

Description

A chicken sandwich is a delicious and versatile meal that features a juicy, tender piece of chicken—grilled, fried, or roasted—nestled between two slices of bread or a soft bun. Often topped with crisp lettuce, fresh tomato, pickles, and a flavorful sauce like mayo or spicy aioli, it offers a satisfying combination of textures and flavors. Whether served plain for a simple bite or loaded with extras like cheese, bacon, or coleslaw, the chicken sandwich has become a staple in fast food and home kitchens alike. Its ability to be customized makes it a favorite for just about any palate.
